Tikka Masala

TTikka is the Hindi term for “small chunks,” and masala means a spice blend. So when small chunks of chicken are cooked in a sauce with a particular spice blend.

Chana (Chole) Masala:

Chana or Chole is the Hindi name for chickpeas. Chana masala is simply chickpeas cooked in an onion, ginger, and garlic-based sauce with garam masala added to it.

Saag Paneer

TSaag is simply the Hindi name for leafy green vegetables like spinach, collard greens, mustard greens, and more. But this particular dish refers to a delicious curry



Korma

Korma is a nutty, creamy curry often made with crushed cashews or almonds. In most korma dishes, protein is cooked with a yogurt-based sauce flavored with ginger and garlic.

Rogan Josh

Rogan Josh: This dish hails from the beautiful northern state of India, Kashmir. With its roots in Persian cuisine, this dish is traditionally cooked with lamb or goat.

Vindaloo

Biryani

A very popular Indian rice dish with its roots in the Mughal empire in India, biryani is a rice dish prepared with several layers of rice, some kind of spiced meat.

TAloo Gobi

A widely loved Indian vegetarian dish made up of spiced cauliflower and potatoes. This is a dish people often make at home because it is easy to make.

Masala Chai
